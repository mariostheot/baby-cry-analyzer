Put the trained model files here to bundle them inside the APK:

  cry_reason.tflite
  yamnet.tflite
  labels.txt
  cry_reason_trainable.tflite   (optional, enables on-device fine-tuning)

You get these by training in Google Colab (see ../../../../ml-training/README.md):
unzip artifacts/model_bundle.zip and copy the files into this folder, then rebuild.

Alternatively (no rebuild needed), copy the same files onto the phone at:
  Android/data/com.babycry.analyzer/files/models/

If no models are found, the app still runs using a rough built-in heuristic.
